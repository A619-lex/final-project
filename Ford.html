FORD SCHEDULED SERVICE
Ford Scheduled Service intervals are specifically tailored to your vehicle type, engine and transmission based on your vehicle's age and mileage, developed and recommended by our expert engineers and carried out by our Ford-trained technicians. Regular servicing means you can minimise the risk of major repair expenses, as well as maintaining the efficiency of your Ford by improving fuel economy, and maintaining the manufacturer's warranty. And if you decide to sell your vehicle, a full service history will enhance its value. Create or log in to your Ford Account and add your vehicle for full details on your Ford Scheduled Service intervals, warranty T&Cs, book a service and much more.
